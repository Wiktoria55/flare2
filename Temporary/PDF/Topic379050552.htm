Pszczyna Castle

The most famous tourist attraction in Pszczyna is the castle. In 2009 it was voted as one of the Seven Architectural Wonders of the Silesian Voivodeship. The castle is characterized by rich, luxurious decor. There are living rooms, an impressive hall of mirrors and an armory with rich equipment. By visiting it, you can admire the conditions in which wealthy people from past eras lived. To visit the Pszczyna castle, you do not have to come to the city and buy tickets - all the interiors can be visited online - here .
