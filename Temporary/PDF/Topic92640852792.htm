Open-Air museum

One of the most popular tourist attractions in Pszczyna is the open-air museum called "Zagroda Wsi Pszczyńskiej". It is an area of 2 hectares in which we can visit several buildings made of wood. The buildings are from Pszczyna and the sourrounding areas were build in the 18th and 19th century. The oldest object is the court granary from Czechowice dating back to 1784. Other interesting building include:

  • an octagonal barn from Kryry (18th c.),
  • a farmhouse from Grzawa (1831),
  • a smithy from Goczałkowice (19th c.).
