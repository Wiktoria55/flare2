The Prince’s Stables

From the castle, we move to the nearby prince stables, which were built in 1864-1867. The stables are a particularly interesting attraction for people fascinated by horses and hunting. You can find exhibitions devoted to the Pszczyna forests and the animals living there - hunting weapons and trophies. You can also see horse-drawn vehicles from the early 20th century.
